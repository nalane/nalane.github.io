Nathaniel Lane

Hello! My name is Nate. I'm a computer scientist currently based in Bozeman, MT. I have a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Colorado School of Mines, where I graduated summa cum laude, and a Master's in Computer Science from Montana State University.

My Master's thesis focused on using recurrent neural networks (RNNs) to identify anti-cancer peptides. I wrote a paper with my advisor, Indika Kahanda, which has been accepted by the International Conference on Practical Applications of Computational Biology & Bioinformatics (PACBB), and is currently pending publication.
